Multichannel‑Localization

This research proposes a novel multichannel approach for localizing camouflaged objects , which are often challenging to detect as they blend seamlessly into their surroundings. The method combines Fourier phase–amplitude analysis with local entropy calculations and integrates them with Class Activation Maps (CAM) and Global Average Pooling (GAP) to highlight discriminative regions. Unlike many deep learning–based solutions, the proposed technique is computationally lightweight, requires no feature training, and is effective even in resource-constrained environments. Experiments on multiple benchmark COD datasets (COD10K, CAMO, CHAMELEON, NC4K) showed that the approach successfully localized over 80% of camouflaged objects, making it a practical and efficient solution for real-world detection tasks.
